Lasagna Recipe

This is a delicious lasagna recipe.

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and parsley.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden on top.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
